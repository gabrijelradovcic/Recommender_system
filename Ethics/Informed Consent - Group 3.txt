You are invited to participate in a research study aimed at improving movie recommendations. By participating, you will interact with a set of algorithms to provide feedback on movie suggestions.

Please be assured that all your responses will be confidential. Your personal information and individual responses will not be shared publicly; they will be used only for the purpose of this research. Any reporting or publishing of the research findings will be in aggregate form, ensuring that individual participants cannot be identified.

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You have the right to withdraw from the study at any point without any repercussions. If you have any concerns or questions before, during, or after participating, please do not hesitate to reach out to our research team.

Thank you for considering being a part of this study. Your feedback and time are invaluable to us.
